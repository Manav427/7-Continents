What is Continent:-

The huge landmasses that are scattered all around the globe. There are total of 7 continents:-
1.Asia
2.North America
3.Europe
4.Africa
5.South America
6.Antartica
7.Australia

*There are total of 195 countries all over the world.

History of continents:-

According to Wegener's theory, Earth's continents once formed a single, giant landmass, which he called Pangaea. Over millions of years, Pangaea slowly broke apart due to the movements of Tectonic plates, eventually forming the continents as they are today.
