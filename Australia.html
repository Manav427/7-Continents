About the Australia(very short)

Australia, the smallest continent and one of the largest countries on Earth, lying between the Pacific and Indian oceans in the Southern Hemisphere. This continent might be small but it has a variety of fauna. It has large population of native people, also referred as Aborigines. The main language of Australia is English.
